Overview

Sales Arena is a real-time gamified leaderboard for the Hitachi Solutions sales team. It lets the team log closed deals, earn points for different package types, and compete on a live leaderboard — all within a clean, simple web interface.

TabWho can accessPurpose
LeaderboardAll usersView rankings, points, revenue and badges
PackagesAll usersBrowse available packages and their point values
AdminAll approved usersManage sales reps, packages and deals
App AdminSuperusers onlyManage user access, approvals and admin rights
Real-time: The leaderboard and activity feed update instantly when a deal is logged — no page refresh needed.
2

Getting Started

2.1 Sign Up

Access is restricted to Hitachi Solutions email addresses. To request access:

  1. Go to the Sales Arena URL provided by your admin
  2. Click Sign up on the login page
  3. Enter your name and company email address (hitachisolutions.com or hsdyn.com)
  4. Submit — your account will be created but access is pending approval
  5. An App Admin will review and approve your request
  6. Once approved, sign in to access the app
Note: You will not be able to access the app until an App Admin approves your account. Contact your admin if approval is taking longer than expected.

2.2 Sign In

Once your account is approved, visit the Sales Arena URL and sign in with your email and password. You will be taken straight to the Leaderboard.

3

User Roles

There are three access levels in Sales Arena:

Pending
  • Signed up but not yet approved
  • Cannot access the app
  • Waiting for App Admin review
Approved User
  • Full leaderboard access
  • Can log deals
  • Can manage reps, packages and deals (Admin tab)
App Admin
  • Everything above
  • App Admin tab access
  • Approve / reject users
  • Grant or revoke admin rights
4

App Navigation

The top navigation bar is always visible and contains the following elements:

  • H logo + Sales Arena — branding, shows the current fiscal year period
  • + Log deal — red button (top right) to open the deal logging form
  • Your initials — shows your account initial
  • Sign out — logs you out of the app

Below the header, four stats cards show a live summary: Deals Closed, Top Earner, Revenue Unlocked, and Days Remaining in the current fiscal year.

Tab navigation sits below the stats bar — click any tab to switch views.

5

Leaderboard

The Leaderboard ranks all sales reps by points earned. Each row shows the rep's rank, name, country flag, achievement badges, number of deals closed, total revenue, and a points bar.

5.1 Filtering by Period

Use the toggle buttons in the top-right of the leaderboard to filter by period:

  • This FY — current financial year (April to March)
  • Prev FY — previous financial year
  • This Month — current calendar month only
  • All Time — every deal ever logged

5.2 Achievement Badges

Reps automatically earn badges based on their performance. Badges appear beneath their name on the leaderboard:

cloud ace streak x5 rising star enterprise
  • Cloud Ace — 5 or more deals closed
  • Streak x N — consecutive deal streak of 5 or more
  • Rising Star — new rep with a deal this quarter
  • Enterprise — £500,000 or more in total revenue

5.3 Activity Feed

Below the leaderboard, the Activity Feed shows a live stream of the most recently logged deals — including the rep's name, package, client, and points earned. It updates in real time whenever a new deal is submitted anywhere in the app.

6

Logging a Deal

Any approved user can log a deal. Click the + Log deal button in the top-right corner of the app to open the form.

  1. Select the Sales Rep who closed the deal from the dropdown
  2. Select the Package — each option shows the point value it awards
  3. Enter the Client Name (e.g. HSBC, NHS Trust, Rolls Royce)
  4. Enter the Deal Value in pounds (£)
  5. Click Log deal to submit

On success, a confirmation screen shows the points awarded. The leaderboard and activity feed update instantly. The modal closes automatically after a moment.

Tip: Points are calculated automatically based on the package selected. Different packages award different point amounts — check the Packages tab for current values.
Note: Deal value must be greater than zero. All fields are required.
7

Packages

The Packages tab shows all available deal packages with their associated point values and colour coding. Each package represents a type of solution sold.

Packages are managed in the Admin tab. Only packages that have no associated deals can be deleted — packages already linked to existing deals are protected.

When logging a deal, the package selection determines how many points the rep earns for that deal.

8

Admin Panel

The Admin tab is available to all approved users and contains three management sections.

8.1 Manage Sales Reps

Add and manage the team members who appear on the leaderboard and in the Log Deal selector.

  • Add a rep — enter full name, job title (optional), and country (UK, France, or Germany), then click + Add rep
  • Edit a rep — click Edit on any row to update their name, job title, or country inline, then click Save
  • Remove a rep — click the ✕ button to permanently remove them
Note: Sales reps are separate from login accounts. A sales rep entry represents the team member tracked on the leaderboard. Login accounts are managed separately in the App Admin tab.

8.2 Manage Packages

Create and maintain the packages available when logging deals.

  • Add a package — enter a name, point value, and choose a colour, then click + Add package
  • Edit a package — click Edit to change name, points, or colour inline
  • Delete a package — only possible if no deals have been logged against it. The delete button is greyed out for packages in use

8.3 Manage Deals

View and edit the 50 most recent deals. Each row shows the deal date, rep name, package, client, value, and points earned.

  • Edit a deal — click Edit to open the edit form. You can change the rep, package, client name, deal value, and date closed. Points are recalculated automatically when you change the package
  • Delete a deal — click ✕ to permanently remove the deal. This updates the leaderboard immediately
Note: Deleting a deal permanently removes it and reduces the rep's points total accordingly. This action cannot be undone.
9

App Admin Panel

The App Admin tab is only visible to superusers. It controls who has access to Sales Arena and what level of access they have.

9.1 Pending Approvals

When a new user signs up, they appear here until approved or rejected.

  • Approve — grants the user access. They can immediately sign in and use the app
  • Reject — permanently removes their account. They would need to sign up again if re-invited
Tip: New sign-up requests do not send automatic email notifications. Check this tab periodically or ask new users to let you know when they have signed up.

9.2 Active Users

Shows all users who currently have access to Sales Arena. Each row displays their name, email, join date, and any app admin badge if applicable.

  • Make admin / Remove admin — grants or revokes App Admin (superuser) rights. Admins gain access to this App Admin tab
  • Revoke — removes access entirely. The user is moved back to the pending queue and loses all rights. Their account data is retained
Safety: You cannot modify your own account from this panel. Your own row has no action buttons, preventing accidental self-lockout.
